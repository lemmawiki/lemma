---
term: halving
flag: |
  Korean '반감기' is a heavily overloaded term — it also means the half-life of radioactive decay (physics). Same word, two completely different domains. The Bitcoin sense is a discrete event; the physics sense is a continuous decay constant.
related: [bitcoin]
---
In Bitcoin: every ~4 years (every 210,000 blocks), the reward for mining a block halves. Built-in disinflation.
